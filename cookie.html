Cookie Policy

This application does not use tracking cookies, advertising scripts, or third-party analytics.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. This includes:

  • Your encrypted Vault PIN
  • Your shared Station PIN
  • Your scan history
  • Hardware preferences (Camera ID)

Production Privacy

No data is ever uploaded to a server. The connection to your station script via Bluetooth/Sharing is handled entirely by your device's native operating system functions.
